## üé• Recording Tips

1. **Screen Resolution:** Use 1920x1080 for best quality
2. **Browser:** Use Chrome or Firefox in a clean profile (no extensions visible)
3. **Speed:** Move deliberately but not too slow
4. **Pauses:** Pause 2-3 seconds on important screens
5. **Mouse Movement:** Smooth, purposeful movements
6. **Cursor:** Consider hiding cursor for cleaner look (optional)
7. **Frame Rate:** Record at 30 or 60 fps
8. **Format:** Export as MP4 (H.264) for compatibility
